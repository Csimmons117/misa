About Us

The Management Information Systems Association (MISA) at CSUN is a student-run, non-profit organization dedicated to preparing students for careers in Information Systems and Technology.

We host weekly meetings, professional workshops, networking opportunities, and guest speakers in Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, IT Audit, and Systems Analysis.
