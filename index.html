About Us

The Management Information Systems Association (MISA) at CSUN is a student-run, non-profit organization dedicated to preparing students for careers in Information Systems and Technology.

We offer weekly meetings, professional workshops, networking opportunities, and guest speakers from industry leaders in Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, IT Audit, Systems Analysis, and more.
