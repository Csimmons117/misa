About Us

Management Information Systems Association (MISA) at the David Nazarian College of Business and Economics at CSUN is a non-profit student organization open to all students interested in the information systems industry. We aspire to showcase the best of what IS/IT has to offer in the business world to empower ambitious students as they transition from the classroom into the industry as professionals. MISA offers beneficial outreach opportunities that enhance a student’s education in the form of weekly meetings with speakers from a variety of employers that present to our students about technical topics and career paths including Data Analytics, Systems Analysis, IT Security, IT Audit, Commercial IT, Tech Support, etc.

We host weekly meetings, professional workshops, networking opportunities, and guest speakers in Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, IT Audit, and Systems Analysis.
